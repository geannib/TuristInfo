In Dobrich is located the largest monument of the Khan Asparuh in Bulgaria. The monument is the workoera of Velichko Minekov and of the architect Ivan Nikolov. It was opened in 1981 to commemorate the 1300 anniversary from the founding of Bulgaria.
Raised in memory of the creator of the first Bulgarian state it is considered an emblematic sign of the town of Dobrich. Every year on March the 3rd, the memorial is the center of festivities during the holiday.
The monument is artistic lighted, the water mirror of the complex is restored and the  pedestrian areas are renovated, being planted bushes with various leaves.
