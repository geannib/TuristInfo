Berchinata Guesthouse It is located 18 km from Belogradchik town in Stakevtsi.
The house has four rooms. Two of them are bedrooms and the third room has a bed and a sofa seat, and the fourth room has two single beds and two folding chairs. It also has a kitchen, restaurant, barbecue, satellite TV, the living room is fully equipped and has a shared bathroom.
The house is suitable for family holidays in every season. Outstanding panoramic views are opened. Hunting and fishing enthusiasts are also welcome. From the river can be fished trout and offers excellent conditions for hunting.
