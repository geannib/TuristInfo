Marina property is located 18 km from the town of Belogradchik in Stakevtsi. It is suitable for 10 people. It has two rooms, in one room are two single beds, and in the other - a double bed and two single beds, a kitchen and a bathroom, fully furnished. The house has another building composed of two rooms with twin beds and bathroom. It has barbecue, table and benches.
The house is suitable for family holidays every season. It has outstanding panoramic views. Hunting and fishing enthusiasts are also welcome. From the river can be fished trout Midzhur state reserve offers excellent conditions for hunting.
