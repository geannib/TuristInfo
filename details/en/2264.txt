This hotel is situated at five minutes from the center of the city Pleven, in the proximity of the center of the city, the administrative area and the divertissement area. You can combine you business trip with pleasant moments. There are 194 available rooms, with a broad range of equipments, 15 apartments, 2 restaurants, bar and a Night club, coffee shop, conference rooms, guarded parking. The guests of this hotel can enjoy a variety of services in order to make their stay more enjoyable.
