Anina house is located 18 km from the town of Belogradchik in Stakevtsi. It is suitable for 11 people. It has five rooms. Two of them are bedrooms, the third with a single bed, the fourth has two single beds and the fifth, a double bed and a double sofa bed. It has a fully equipped interior, summer kitchen with a warehouse and wood for barbecue, two bathrooms, satellite TV. The house is suitable for holidays and family vacations in every season. The price per night is 20 leva per person for the summer season and 25 leva for the winter season.
