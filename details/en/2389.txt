The complex nature protection "Lomovete" was built with funds from the Environment Operational Programme 2007-2013 for the "Lomovete - center for the preservation of birds who make their nests in stone" and equipped with the project "Sustainable Development Activities Nature Park "Rusenski Lom". The visitor center through multimedia, can see the exhibition protected birds, attractions and rich biodiversity of the park and its historical cultural heritage. It has separate places for training and seminars. Cells were built for temporary accommodation of animals in distress and a small laboratory. The complex is at the heart of the natural park, about 100 m from the confluence of two rivers (Mali Lom and Beli Lom), close to s. Nisovo, and from this start many tourist routes.
