It is created in 2001 near the “Ledenika”cave and becomes one of the most visited places on the territory of Vratsa town. It is mostly preferred by families, schoolers, and individual tourists. The new design of the eco path will surprise its visitors with new fairytale characters, stories with unexpected end, and entertaining games. There is also good news for the smallest visitors of the attraction, there are no bad animals, unlike the fairytale heroes. The bear, the wolf and the snake has their part in the forest system, all of them are helping for keeping the nature’s balance. On the route you can see the “House of Baba Yaga”, the house of the fairytale heroes and etc.
