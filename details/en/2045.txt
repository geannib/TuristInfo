It is located in a municipal building with local cultural significance, known as "Old School" or "The Pension". The massive stone building dating from the early century. XX and is characterized by monumental architecture and high aesthetic value. The building and its surrounding park were renovated and adapted to new needs and specific functions of the educational center for promotional activities for environmental conservation and tourism.
The center is multifunctional and has classrooms, halls for exhibitions representing biodiversity and cultural and historical heritage of the area, internet and IT system for online wetlands monitoring through cameras installed in appropriate places. In addition to direct observation of our lakes by the visitors, IT system is in support for the control bodies that monitors compliance with environmental legislation.
In the adjacent park area of the center there are detached and several recreation areas that expose vegetation typical of the area: the lake with aquatic vegetation and rocky area with a rock garden.
