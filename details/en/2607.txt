Florentiana medieval fortress is located about 1 km west of the village of Florentin, in the "Valley of the Turks", near the Danube. It is assumed that the fortress was built in XII-XIII century and was well preserved until the early nineteenth century. The city entered into the Vidin's Kingdom of Ivan Sratsimir.

Fortification walls were built of stone cemented with white mortar mixed with gravel. Today, from the castle remained only a small part of the foundation of a wall.
