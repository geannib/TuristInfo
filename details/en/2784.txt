The trail starts at the end of Zgorigrad village and follows the Canyon of Leva River. A climbing system of bridges and stairs on the both slopes of river gets to the “Borov Kamak” Falls, which soars some 63 meters. The trail passes under the waterfall and goes up by wooden stair on the right side. Above the waterfall you will find beautiful green meadows with shelter and barbeque area.
