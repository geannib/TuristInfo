Chuprene phage is a protected area in the district of Vidin located in villages Stakevtsi, Gorni Lom, and Chuprene Repleana, Vidin.
On  October 2nd, 1986 has been established the buffer zone of the reserve "Chuprene" on an area of 542.3 hectares, which on 12th of July 2007 by order of Minister Chakarov is classified as a protected area. On 12th of  March 2012, the area was increased to 553.06 hectares.
