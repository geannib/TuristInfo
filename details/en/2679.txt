Since opening in 1970, base of canoeing in Vidin is one of the most modern training facilities for rowers in Bulgaria. Over the past seven years, Bulgarian rowers from Vidin, have won 130 medals. Athletes successes are visible to everyone, but unfortunately now the state of the sports complex is extremely worse. However, every day depending on the train, come many children, who continue the traditions of Kayaking  School in Vidin.
