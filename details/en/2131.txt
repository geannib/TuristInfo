The Romantic Boulevard with Sycamore trees is a pedestrian zone, about 2 km long. The trees are old almost, 100ani with large crowns, in the shape of green tunnel, in downtown of Vârşeţ, near the neighborhood Zanozhene. Since the beginning of last century, it was, in a traditional way a place for walks. On both sides of the boulevard are some of the most beautiful villas in the city, built in the early twentieth century. Near the boulevard is Giganteya Sequoia, which has been declared a natural landmark.
