 
For lovers of sport fishing, the Dam "Plakovo" gives them the opportunity to try their luck. Located at the edge of the northwestern Plakovopevalea village and  Elishan River, between the roads coming to the village, from Kilifarevo city and Debelets city. The Lake has a very dense population of carp, with an average weight of 2-3 kg.. 
 
The dam is accessible by  Veliko Tarnovo road - Debelets - Elena. Even before Plakovo village is a gravel road  indicated with the inscription - "angling".
