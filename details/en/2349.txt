Natural monument "Dikilitash" or "standing stone" is on the Maule river Danube, about 2 km north of s. Mechka, mun. Ivanovo, Ruse, in the Stalbishte. Is rocky formations of different sizes (2.5 and 12 m), probably dating back to the Mesozoic era. For they bind legends and myths of Marko Mountains, which raised a large stone on his little finger, which led to its supranumirea - "Stone Mountains Marko". There are assumptions that the great stone was used as a watchtower by the Romans and is part of the remains of a bridge crossing the Danube River.
