Wonderful holiday houses are located in Brest, 36 km from Vratsa. They offer free Wi-Fi, barbecue and free private parking.
The houses have a patio, air conditioning and seating and dining. Kitchens are equipped with dishwasher, oven, toaster, refrigerator, hob and kettle. Each house includes a private bathroom with hairdryer. It is provided bedding. Guests can play table tennis or hire bikes. Sofia Airport is 80 km from the holiday homes Wonderful.
