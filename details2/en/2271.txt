Villa Kaylaka is located in the nature reserve Kaylaka Park and offers views of the lake and free outdoor pool and spa with sauna. Also provides free Wi-Fi internet access and free private parking. All rooms have flat-screen TV with satellite channels, balcony and seating area. They include a private bathroom with shower and free toiletries. Each room overlooks the lake and mountains.
Villa Kaylaka offers a la carte restaurant. Upon request breakfast can be served in the room. Upon request and for an extra fee the hotel organizes visits to the cave-wine museum and tours by plane and hang glider with an instructor and an option for video.
Free bikes are available to guests during their stay. The center of Pleven can be reached within 7 minutes by car.
