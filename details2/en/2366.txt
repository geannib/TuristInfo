Rope bridge over the river Yantra Belyanovo the Village was built in 1968. It is located west of the village. It has a lungume 105 meters and was the longest bridge in Bulgaria until 2001, when it was built in the village Lisitsite bridge, linking two sides of the lake. Studen Kladenets, near the town of Kardzhali.

The bridge links the village Novgrad Belyanovo village. At the end of the bridge is carried out traditional camps for trips on the Danube River Yantra direction, inland river kayaking is one of the ways of arriving at the bridge. Near the can and meadow camping there built a rest area with gazebo, tables and benches.
