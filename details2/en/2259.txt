The “Gergana” zoo, attracts more visitors with its well kept lawns, a variety of exotic animals, recreations and a playground. It is situated at 6 kilometers from the center of the village Gostilea, in the direction of Pleven. The area, occupies approximately 5 – 5, 6 hectares and the caring of the animals started in the year 1980. No, there can be observed: the majestic lion Aybar, the black swans, wild geese, boars, deers, the Wolf pony, the peacock with the beautiful tail of feathers, quails, pheasants, the diversity is big!
