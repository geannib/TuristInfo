Hotel "Tachev" is a three star hotel located in the city. Cherven Bryag Cherven Bryag municipality. Set on four floors with 15 rooms and 4 suites.
Each room has a private bathroom, cable TV, wireless internet, balcony, central heating and air conditioning. The hotel will welcome you with a lobby bar and restaurant. It has a modern conference hall equipped with a multimedia screen.
It has its own parking. Near the hotel is situated summer pool.
