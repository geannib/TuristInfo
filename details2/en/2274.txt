The Hotel Prestige at Belene is situated at 300 meters from the center and it offers rooms with free access to Wi-Fi. It offers free access to the seasonal outdoor pool, a fitness center and a private parking.
Most of the rooms from Prestige are equipped with air conditioning. Each room has cable TV, a relaxation area and a refrigerator. Some of the unities have a well equipped kitchenette and a balcony with a view towards the river. The bathroom is equipped with a shower or a bath tub and free toilet articles. There is a commune balcony.
The guests can have a meal at the restaurant from the premise. At the request and for a tax there are offered ATVs with a guide.
At 200 meters from the Hotel Prestige there is situated a food store. At two minutes of walking there is a bus stop.
