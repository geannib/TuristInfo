The recreation area "Steel" was created in 1981 to celebrate 1300 years since the establishment of the Bulgarian state. Built over 2.5 meters high from pots capturing the water coming from the middle. Next to it there is a reproduction of the Bulgarian map and the year 1300 both drawn with bushes. The symbols are very beautiful seen from the main road especially in spring when the bushes blossom. The idea belongs to Eng. Stefan Boyadzhiev, manager of the local roads, which are known in the region for their beautiful plants. The area is often visited by locals and those passing through.
