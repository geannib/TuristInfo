The complex has one of the most modern facilities for horses. Their indoor arena  enables riding, throughout the year. Care, food and accommodation of horses are at  extremely high levels. The large opened space (paddock) of base Riding allow animals to spend free time in conditions close to natural ones.
Shooting and horse riding base "Recar"  are located in Dobrich city and it is a wonderful place where you can escape the busy life.
The resort offers excellent conditions for recreation, meeting with friends, target shooting, paintball, horseback riding, pony riding, barbecue, birthdays, holidays for children and adults, teambuilding and others.
