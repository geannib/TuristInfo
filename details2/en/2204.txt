On the land onto which is built the sports complex, there are two distinct areas - the stadium and the sports center with swimming pool.
  - The stadium consists of a football field, track and Rostrums for spectators.
  - Multifunctional sports hall is designed for volleyball, basketball, handball, tennis, and is able to be used for other sports and events.
   - The pool is part of the gym. It has the dimensions of 25 / 12,50 meters.
