This trail was built in Gradishte locality, in the immediate vicinity of Vratsata Gorge, where the city of Vratsa was once established according to historical records. At the time of the second Bulgarian state the settlement was forti ed with a wall and grew up to be a town center e trail familiarizes visitors with the archaeological excavations, with the functions of each of the sites and their historical and cultural importance. Visitors are shown photos of the tools and jewels found on the site.
