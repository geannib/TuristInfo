The park "Bright Garden" was built in 1922 and in 1930 was electrified. The name of the garden comes from its alleys constructed under the special form of sun rays.
During the years of the Second World War was a place for rich Bulgarians and foreign guests who came mainly on the Danube to meet them. The park was, also visited by Prince Cyril.
The park is located in the city center and is part of the architectural ensemble Banite - Park "Sunny Garden"
