The Hotel Mirage, Pleven disposes of 32 local accommodation places. Situated in the center of the city Pleven, on the street Vasil Levski no. 120 above the fashion shop fro men “Teodor”, on the traffic area of shopping and at 50 m. from the Drama Theatre.
The rooms are equipped with 2 or 3 beds, and an apartment with a separate bedroom. These have their own bathroom, plasma TV, internet, refrigerator and flat iron.
For our guests it is also offered a free parking, which is situated on 37 Dojran street in the courtyard of the hotel Mirage.
