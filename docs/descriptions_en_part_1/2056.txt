Dobrich Karting track is situated in the playground at school OTME. It has a total of 7 cars - 5 adults, 1 child and 1 for professional sports. Maximum speed that develops is 80 km / h. The only requirement for drivers is to be turned at least six years. Track length is 600 m. It is fully insured tires, which protects against injuries. The track is illuminated. Each client comes first go through mandatory instructions to guarantee its security. Carting track has a timing system that shows the results of each cart, average speed and other indicators.
