Church "Sv. Nikolay", known as the "Latin Church" is located on a small height in the center of Barziya. The climb to it going from the square in front of the municipality. According to the church was built "sometime in the XVI century". In XIX century, Due to population growth, naos (salon) the church was extended by six meters. This is probably happen around 1865, as preserved chalice bears dated 9 March 1866. The church was remade during the twentieth century. The church is a basilica, originally south wall had been dug into the rock and had no windows.
