Natural Park "Oak" or park "Kobaklaka" as it is known by the residents of Dobrich is located 6 km from the city on an area of 3700 acres. The park consists mainly of oak forests. Translated from Turkish name means artificially planted forest Oaks.
Today, the park is a favorite place for the citizens of Dobrich where to relax, walk and rest. Here are built several playgrounds for children, sport and farm pheasants. In the park there are two restaurants where are often held banquets and weddings.
The park is located outside the town of Dobrich, but is easily accessible by car, going on the road to s. Odrintsi.
Longer rides enthusiasts can reach Kobaklaka and walk downtown Dobrich. The walk up there takes about two hours and passes through trails in the woods.
