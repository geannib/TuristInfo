With the construction of the dome completed the rough construction of the new church in the regional town. Construction of the church began in 2007, as for the period there was built the foundations and the crypt. In the following years were raised the walls - basement, ground floor, porch, the bell tower too. The funds are owned or target of the municipality. Total area of the church is 1088 square. Between 550 and 600 lay people can gather inside. A park area of 16 acres will be built around the church.
