It is situated in the town bearing the same name and it is 18 m high. It is and environmentally protected area which draws of tourist, either Bulgarian people or foreigners, keen on admiring spectacular natural monuments. It can be reached by eco path "Damyanitca", which starts from a break down asphalt road in the town. To find the road, better ask the locals. The eco path begins with a dirt road, climbing gradually to the woods and mountains. Signs are placed at key locations, indicating direction of the waterfall. To the waterfall takes about 1.30 hours walk in one direction as in the upper part of the eco-trail runs along the valley of the river "Stara reka" and it is quite steep.
