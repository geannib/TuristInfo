The SPA Hotel "Minkovi Baths" is located in Spancevţi, Montana, in a mountainous area on the west of the mountain, at the foothills of the Mountain Teddy Dolls. The nearest town is Varshetz at only 5 km away, and the mountain Todorini Dolls is located at 2 km away from the village Spancevţi, Montana. The spa hotel offers accommodation only in hotels and mountain cottages. The hotel spa is open all year round. There is a restaurant and an outdoor swimming pool, tennis court and mini football court, billiards. In the spa area are available treatments like - sauna, steam room, jacuzzi for two, relaxing cabins, solarium, massage dry tangentor, water massage, laconium.
