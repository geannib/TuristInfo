A steep pathway leads from the village square up to Presvetitsa locality to the north end of the village, where vestiges of an early Christian church of the fifth or sixth century has been unearthed, climbing further up on the road through Rachova Poliana to Padesh locality. Now the trail takes to the north on the ridge of Tomovski hillside, passing through Goren Kliuch locality with a small stone shelter. Crossing the east side of Mount Ostra leads to the broad Kuzmanishki Hollow. On the left , the cliff face of Kobilini Steni is seen and on the right, Mount Beglichka Mogila, the highest peak of the Mountain of Vratsa. A 30-minute walk down takes you to Parshevitsa Hut.
